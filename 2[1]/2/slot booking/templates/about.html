Welcome to VOMC

VOMC (Volunteer Of Mahajana Cops) is a college-based initiative dedicated to supporting and coordinating volunteer efforts. Our volunteers actively participate in college events, cultural functions, and extend their service to community-based activities, contributing to social well-being.

Our Vision

To foster a spirit of service, leadership, and teamwork among students by promoting volunteerism for the betterment of our society.

Our Mission

  • Support college events by providing efficient volunteer management.
  • Encourage students to engage in community service programs.
  • Develop leadership and organizational skills among volunteers.
  • Create a positive impact on society through meaningful initiatives.

Why Join VOMC?

By joining VOMC, you get a chance to contribute to society, enhance your leadership abilities, and gain hands-on experience in event management. Whether it’s a college function or a community service project, every effort you make leaves a lasting impact.
